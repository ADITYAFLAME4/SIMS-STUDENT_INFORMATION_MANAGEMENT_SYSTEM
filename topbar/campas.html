TECHNO INDIA- CHANDRA SEKHAR ACADEMY IT TRAINING CENTRE

From early days I could understand that a child should be trained as per the need of its society. In the year 2004 I started a CBSE school in the name Chandra Sekhar Academy in Puri, Odisha . There would be a bridge between Japan & India, I thought, if Japanese language & culture could be introduced in my school. Accordingly I made it stand distinctive being the only school in Odisha where Japanese language and culture has been taught as an elective subject from LKG to Senior Secondary (+2) level now. More so , the school has opened its international branch in Kyoto , the former capital of Japan in the year 2018 as a goodwill gesture to help out the children of Indian as well as its peripheral expats in an affordable fee structure those who will be coming to Japan . The school holds the proud legacy of becoming the first ever such Indian based English medium school in Kansai region, western Japan. Japan is a small but technologically advanced country & in Japan India is known for its IT. Realising its importance , in the year 2019 I joined hands with Techno India university , one of the premier universities of eastern India in West Bengal, Kolkata & made a humble beginning of the IT training centre in the name Techno India-Chandra Sekhar Academy in Puri, Odisha to educate my students those who opt for an IT career after completion of +2 or undergoing Graduate (Final Year) or Graduate students of Puri as well as Odisha, India by introducing various computer related courses along with the implementation of first ever such course in Puri as O level & A level NIELIT course under the Ministry of Electronics & IT Govt. of India . This has become the only IT centre in the country where Japanese language & culture is imparted with its designated IT course. My dream was to put Indian youths in Japanese IT companies . The A level designed for one and half years has its global acceptance especially by the Japanese companies in India and Japan as well. Japanese language & culture has been put as an addition to make our interns more candid in their workplaces. We have joined hands with KCCS Career Tech Inc. (Japan ), a Kyocera group company for the successful engagement of our students as IT professionals after completion of this A level NIELIT course. The company not only engages but also trains our students with adequate technical skills proportionate to the skills required in Japanese IT companies. We also invite children and relatives of NRIs those who intend to come to Japan may have this IT course with Japanese language & culture prior to their visit for a lucrative career in Japanese IT companies.

We put the best of our efforts to match transforming our curricula with the adequate enhancement of skill according to the needs of our workplace and if possible students are appropriately re-educated to overcome the mismatch if any. I would like to appeal all our educated youths to have this golden opportunity and make us feel proud to serve you the best while serving for the nation together.

Hopefully, the effort could be having its prospect to strengthen our business tie-ups attracting more Japanese investors to India and there by make it rich & prosperous nurturing a world culture and making Puri an IT hub for the Millennium.

Techno India Corporate Office

Techno India Group (TIG) is one of the largest knowledge management groups in India, dedicated to the cause of development of Education, Research and Entrepreneurship in Science, Technology, Management and Medicine and has a mission to support the entire knowledge path from Nursery to Ph.D. Techno India Group, commenced its operations way back in 1983 and has now evolved into a constellation of knowledge campuses spread across various geographical locations of the country. The Group is destined to play a pivotal role in delivering quality education, through these knowledge campuses from nursery level to the postgraduate and doctoral level, thereby producing country's best human resource in the fields of Science, Technology, Medicine and Management.
